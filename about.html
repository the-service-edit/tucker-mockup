Our Story · Casuarina, NSW · Est. 2018

More than a café.A community hub.

Great coffee, honest food, and a place that feels like home — built in the heart of Casuarina, a stone's throw from the beach.

How it started

Three mates,one good idea.

Back in 2018, three mates with different skills but one shared obsession — Australian coffee culture — set out to build something Casuarina didn't have yet. Tucker was born: a café built on passion and a simple dream to celebrate everything we love about Aussie hospitality.

Nearly seven years on, we've served a lot of coffee, plated a lot of good food, and become the community hub we always hoped we'd be. The rest, as they say, is history.

What drives us

The things we won't compromise on

High Value
From the plate to the service, we sweat the details so every visit's worth it. Nothing leaves the pass we wouldn't happily eat ourselves.
Local & Sustainable
Locally sourced where we can, made in-house wherever possible. Australian classics with a modern twist — and long-standing relationships with the suppliers we trust.
Casual Comfort
A laid-back room where everyone's welcome — dogs included. Pull up a chair, settle in, and let us take care of the rest.
Meet the team

Phil & Dalethe heart of Tucker.

At the heart of Tucker are Phil and Dale — a team who bring warmth, energy and genuine passion to everything they do.

Together they've built a place driven by a love for great food, great coffee, and the kind of connections that turn first-timers into regulars and regulars into family.

A community hub

We're proud to be more than a café.

Tucker is a place where people gather, friendships form and stories are shared. Whether we're backing local junior sports teams, hosting fundraisers, or giving local teens their first job, we're here to support the community that's supported us — every step of the way.
